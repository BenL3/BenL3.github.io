Summary

My name is Ben Lepsch I'm a Computer Science and Engineering Major at the University of Iowa. My roles on team CHOPS are hardware, embedded systems, and computer engineering vision engineer. Outside of senior design I currently have encountered a lot of free time, but previously worked at Collins Aerospace as a part time employee. I frequently make trips up to the Mississippi to go wing dam fishing during the Spring and Summer.
